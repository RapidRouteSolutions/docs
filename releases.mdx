# What's New

The latest formats and improvements in RapidEPR. Newest releases are listed first.

## April 2026

### Air Force OPB/EPB Form-Aware Formats

Air Force users now have two new form-aware options for current OPB and EPB work:

- **OPB Section (Officer)** — choose the exact AF Form 715 / myEval OPB section you are writing, including Duty Description, Rater Stratification, the four Major Performance Areas, Mandatory Comments, HLR Stratification, HLR Performance Statement, and HLR Assignment / Developmental Education Recommendation.
- **EPB Section (Enlisted)** — choose the exact AF Form 716 / myEval EPB section and rated grade. Supports Duty Description, the four Major Performance Areas, Mandatory Comments, MSgt-SMSgt Stratification, Future Roles, HLR Comments, and CMSgt Higher Responsibility Support.

The existing Air Force options are still available, but the form-aware OPB/EPB options are the best starting point when you know where the text will be pasted.

[Try an Air Force format →](https://rapidepr.com/bullets) — see the [Air Force examples](/bullet-generator/examples/usaf) for current format names and sample output.

### Army NCOER and OER Tailored Updates

Army tailored formats are now split by grade tier so output matches the right evaluation form and level of responsibility:

- **NCOER - Tailored (SGT)** — direct-level SGT support.
- **NCOER - Tailored (SSG-1SG)** — organizational-level SSG, SFC, MSG, and 1SG support.
- **NCOER - Tailored (CSM/SGM)** — strategic-level CSM and SGM support.
- **OER - Tailored (Company Grade)** — DA Form 67-10-1, 2LT through CPT.
- **OER - Tailored (Field Grade)** — DA Form 67-10-2, MAJ through LTC.
- **OER - Tailored (Strategic / Colonel)** — DA Form 67-10-3, COL.

The older **NCOER - Tailored (Legacy)** option stays available for users who prefer the previous broad workflow.

[Try an Army tailored format →](https://rapidepr.com/bullets) — see the [Army examples](/bullet-generator/examples/army) for sample output from each grade tier.

### Navy FITREP Tailored, EVAL Tailored, and Chief Eval Summary

Navy users now have section-aware options for enlisted EVALs, officer FITREPs, and Chief Eval summaries:

- **FITREP - Tailored (W2-O6)** — write to the exact section of NAVPERS 1610/2, including Block 28 Command Employment, Block 29 Duties, trait-support comments, Block 40 Career Recommendations, Block 41 Comments on Performance, and GRGB Commentary for CDR / CAPT FITREPs.
- **EVAL - Tailored (E1-E6)** — write to the exact section of NAVPERS 1616/26, including trait-support comments, Block 41 Career Recommendations, Block 43 Comments on Performance, and Block 44 Qualifications / Achievements.
- **Chief Eval Summary (Block 40)** — focused Block 40 Reporting Senior Comments on Performance for NAVPERS 1616/27.

[Try a Navy format →](https://rapidepr.com/bullets) — see the [Navy examples](/bullet-generator/examples/navy) for sample output from each format.
