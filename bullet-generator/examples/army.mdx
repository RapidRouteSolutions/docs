RapidEPR supports both quick Army statements and form-aware NCOER/OER options. The form-aware options are the best choice when you know the rated Soldier's grade tier and the section you are filling.

## Current Army Formats

**Form-Aware**

- **NCOER - Tailored (SGT)** — SGT / direct-level NCOER sections.
- **NCOER - Tailored (SSG-1SG)** — SSG, SFC, MSG, and 1SG organizational-level NCOER sections.
- **NCOER - Tailored (CSM/SGM)** — CSM and SGM strategic-level NCOER sections.
- **OER - Tailored (Company Grade)** — DA Form 67-10-1, 2LT through CPT.
- **OER - Tailored (Field Grade)** — DA Form 67-10-2, MAJ through LTC.
- **OER - Tailored (Strategic / Colonel)** — DA Form 67-10-3, COL.

**General**

- **NCOER** — quick NCOER bullet.
- **NCOER - Tailored (Legacy)** — the previous broad NCOER tailored option.
- **OER** — quick OER narrative statement.

**Other**

- **Senior Rater Comment**
- **Award Achievement**

## NCOER - Tailored (SGT)

Use this for SGT NCOER support. Pick the section you are writing for: Character, Presence, Intellect, Leads, Develops, Achieves, or Rater Overall Performance. The output uses NCOER bullet style and includes `xx` placeholders when numbers are missing.

**Example Input:**
SGT led a 4-Soldier team through 18 training events, certified 3 Soldiers, and improved squad readiness.

**Section:** Rater Overall Performance

**Example Output (Option 1):**
o led 4-Soldier team through 18 training events; certified 3 Soldiers and improved squad readiness by xx%, prepared for increased team leader responsibility

## NCOER - Tailored (SSG-1SG)

Use this for SSG, SFC, MSG, and 1SG NCOER support. Select the rated rank and the section so the tone matches the Soldier's level of responsibility.

**Example Input:**
SSG managed platoon maintenance tracker, trained 12 Soldiers, closed 27 overdue services, and improved equipment readiness from 82% to 94%.

**Rated Rank:** SSG

**Section:** Achieves

**Example Output (Option 1):**
o managed platoon tracker for 27 overdue services; trained 12 Soldiers and raised equipment readiness from 82% to 94% during reporting period

## NCOER - Tailored (CSM/SGM)

Use this for CSM and SGM evaluations. Sections include Character, Attributes and Competencies Narrative, and Rater Overall Performance. Output is broader and more strategic than lower-grade NCOER bullets.

**Example Input:**
CSM advised battalion command team during deployment, synchronized 6 companies, mentored 18 senior NCOs, and improved sponsorship completion from 70% to 96%.

**Rated Rank:** CSM

**Section:** Attributes and Competencies Narrative

**Example Output (Option 1):**
Advised battalion command team across 6 deployed companies while mentoring 18 senior NCOs and raising sponsorship completion from 70% to 96%. Strengthened standards, leader development, and disciplined execution across the formation.

## NCOER

Use this for a quick NCOER-style bullet when you do not need a specific form-aware section.

**Example Input:**
NCO developed a training tracker that helped supervisors close overdue requirements.

**Example Output:**
o created training tracker; closed xx overdue requirements and improved section completion by xx% across xx Soldiers

## NCOER - Tailored (Legacy)

This is the previous broad NCOER tailored option. It is still available for users who prefer the older workflow, but the grade-specific NCOER formats above are usually more precise.

**Example Input:**
NCO led squad through field training and mentored junior Soldiers.

**Section:** Leads

**Example Output (Option 1):**
o led xx-Soldier squad through xx field events; coached xx junior Soldiers and improved team execution across xx mission tasks

## OER - Tailored (Company Grade)

Built for **DA Form 67-10-1**, company-grade officers (2LT-CPT). Section-aware options include Character, Presence, Intellect, Leads, Develops, Achieves, Significant Duties and Responsibilities, Overall Performance Comments, and Senior Rater Comments on Potential.

**Example Input:**
LT served as company XO during NTC rotation, planned 14 logistics packages, and trained 6 new lieutenants.

**Section:** Leads

**Example Output (Option 1):**
LT [name] led with calm, decisive judgment during a demanding NTC rotation; coordinated 14 logistics packages across supported platoons, built training plans for 6 new lieutenants, and reinforced standards that improved company-level execution.

## OER - Tailored (Field Grade)

Built for **DA Form 67-10-2**, field-grade officers (MAJ-LTC). It adds field-grade surfaces such as Performance Narrative, Operational Assignment recommendations, and Broadening Assignment recommendations.

**Example Input:**
MAJ served as battalion S3 during JRTC rotation, synchronized 5 maneuver companies, and built the operations rhythm that produced 12 mission successes.

**Section:** Performance Narrative (Part IV.d.2)

**Example Output (Option 1):**
MAJ [name] performed superbly as battalion S3 throughout a complex JRTC rotation; synchronized 5 maneuver companies, integrated enablers across warfighting functions, and built an operations rhythm that produced 12 mission successes and positioned the battalion for follow-on tasking.

## OER - Tailored (Strategic / Colonel)

Built for **DA Form 67-10-3**, strategic-grade and Colonel-level officers. It emphasizes strategic context, large-formation leadership, combined / joint / interagency complexity, and potential for senior strategic responsibility.

**Example Input:**
COL commanded brigade through CTC rotation, integrated joint and multinational forces, and built a leader development program for 24 field-grade officers.

**Section:** Performance and Potential Narrative (Part IV.c.2)

**Example Output (Option 1):**
COL [name] delivered exceptional brigade command through a complex CTC rotation, integrating joint and multinational forces across warfighting functions and building a leader development program that prepared 24 field-grade officers for follow-on command. Strategic judgment, presence, and proven ability to lead at echelon support continued growth toward senior responsibility.

## OER

Use this for a quick OER-style narrative when you do not need a grade-tiered form section.

**Example Input:**
Officer built a digital tracker that reduced staff reporting delays.

**Example Output:**
[name] applied sound judgment and technical skill to build a digital tracker that reduced staff reporting delays by xx%, improved visibility across xx sections, and gave commanders a clearer tool for managing recurring requirements.

## Senior Rater Comment

Use this for senior-rater potential language. Provide factual rankings, comparison groups, and recommendation context when you have them. RapidEPR will use placeholders instead of inventing unsupported rankings or promotion language.

**Example Input:**
Top 10% of 24 captains I senior rate; ready for company command and resident PME.

**Example Output:**
Top 10% of 24 captains I senior rate. Ready for company command and resident PME; continue to place in positions requiring disciplined leadership, sound judgment, and measurable results.

## Award Achievement

Use this for Army award achievement narratives, such as DA Form 638 Block 21. Include the achievement, scope, dates if relevant, measurable impact, and mission effect.

**Example Input:**
Soldier built tracker that reduced overdue training and improved reporting accuracy.

**Example Output:**
Built a training tracker that closed xx overdue requirements, improved reporting accuracy by xx%, and gave leaders reliable visibility across xx Soldiers. The system reduced missed suspenses and strengthened the unit's ability to manage recurring training requirements.
